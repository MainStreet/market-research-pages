Executive Summary

In this conversation, Doug O'Laughlin and Val discuss the rapidly maturing KV cache and context memory storage market, which has evolved significantly since their previous discussion. The explosion in agentic AI demand has driven organizations to consume 500 million to 1 billion tokens daily, making efficient memory management critical. Jensen's recent CES endorsement of "context memory storage" has validated the market and extended discussion beyond traditional HBM/DRAM to include NVMe storage solutions. Pricing considerations—such as OpenAI's $225 per million output tokens for contexts above 200,000 tokens—are shaping adoption strategies, with organizations increasingly standardizing on 1-million-token context windows despite emerging context rot challenges.
