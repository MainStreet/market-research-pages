Methodology

Portfolio variance = wᵀ Σ w where Σ is the daily covariance matrix of simple returns for tradable positions and w is the weight vector from the source CSV. Cash / money-market / delisted positions are detected and contribute 0 to variance but are still counted in the total portfolio weight, so the annualised std dev reflects the cash drag on realised portfolio volatility.
